About Us

Stance Data mission is to make our client's work simpler, delighful and human. We're a fast growing company in Business Process Outsourcing(BPO) sector. We employ proactive, energetic and ambitious individuals to help our clients reach new heights.

Philosophy

Leveraging our collective experience, we provide a unique experience through our services. Not satisfied with making solutions that simply work; we go above and beyond our clients demands to put a smile on their faces. Our innovative, open, collaborative environment views all challenges as opportunities.

Values

01

Bold Service

It seems simple enough. If we are to succeed, then we'd be smart to promote an environment where the best ideas have a chance to live.

02

Radical Openness

Because trust is built on honesty and openness, we don’t tiptoe around issues when it comes to people’s data. We face them head-on, for better or worse.

05

06

Merit Based Culture

Most folks work more than they play. So if your work life sucks, doesn't that mean the majority of your life sucks? We like less suck, unless you're vacuuming.
